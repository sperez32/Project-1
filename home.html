Tapduc? What even is it?


Tapduc is the Vietnamese word for "Workout". But is also a fitness app that queues music that best matches your exertion level without the need of opening your phone! Tapduc uses the heart rate measurements collected by an user's heart rate and queues a song from your Spotify playlist that best matches your Beats Per Minute.
